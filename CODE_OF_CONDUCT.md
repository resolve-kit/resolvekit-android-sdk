# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors to collaborate in a respectful, constructive way. Feedback should stay focused on code, behavior, and outcomes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, discriminatory language, deliberate disruption, and sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close threads, restrict participation, or ban contributors when behavior harms the project or participants.

For code-of-conduct reports, contact the maintainers through the repository's private contact path.
For security vulnerabilities, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
